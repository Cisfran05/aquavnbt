Leveraged Finance

Need capital to acquire or merge with a business?

Current Client *

We specialize in business acquisition financing

Backed by years of experience, our commercial bаոkers specialize in working with companies to help them navigate the nuances of the merger and acquisition process in California.

Ready to grow your business?

The Leveraged Finance Group is a division of Zions Bancorporation that specializes in buyouts, recapitalizations, and growth capital. We support the space through direct debt financing and fund leverage facilities to support our private equity and credit investment partners. Leveraging the right financial experience to help guide the transaction, a successful merger or acquisition can provide several key advantages like reduced costs, greater synergies, higher growth, and increased valuation. Our partners rely on us for transparent communication, fast execution, and certainty to close.

Decades of Experience

For nearly 70 years, Van Ness Bаnk & Trust has been helping California businesses grow and prosper. With our demonstrated track record of success, you can count on us to help your business merge with or acquire another company.

Decisions Made Locally

We’re deeply involved with the California business community, which allows us to evaluate your opportunities and present you with other sources of equity, while connecting you with a network of resources to help facilitate your transaction.

A Personalized Approach

We understand that every business is unique, and no two transactions are the same. That’s why our knowledgeable and proactive bаոkers are committed to getting to know you and your business – so they can provide you with truly customized solutions.
